Frequently Asked Questions

This website serves as a platform to manage and share church-related information, resources, events, and sermons.

To register, click on the 'Sign Up' button at the top right corner of the homepage and fill in the required details.

You can make a payment via the M-Pesa paybill number provided on the payment page.

Admins can upload sermons by navigating to the admin panel, selecting the 'Sermons' section, and filling out the upload form with the sermon details and media.

Admins can post events by accessing the 'Events' section in the admin panel, where they can fill out the event form with the necessary details and submit it for publication.

Users can view or download church resources by navigating to the 'Resources' section on the website, selecting the desired category, and clicking on the resource they want to view or download.

If you have forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

You can contact support by navigating to the 'Contact' page and filling out the contact form with your query or by emailing us directly at support@churchwebsite.com.
