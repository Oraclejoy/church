Vision

Faith Church's vision is to be a vibrant community passionately loving God, people, and transforming lives, families, and communities through Christ's power.

Mission

Faith Church's mission is to make disciples, baptize, and teach obedience to Christ's commands. We equip believers, nurture faith, and share Christ's love locally and globally.

Goals

Faith Church's goal is to create a welcoming environment where people can grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and serve others with love and compassion, reflecting the character of Christ in all we do.
